Noisy Colouring Book

An interactive colouring book with sounds for children. Choose a picture, pick colours, and tap shapes to fill them in. Every colour plays its own sound!

Getting Started

  1. Choose a category from the start screen (Scenes, Simple, Animals, etc.).
  2. The first picture in that category loads automatically.
  3. Tap or click on a colour in the palette on the right.
  4. Tap or click on any shape in the picture to fill it with that colour.
  5. When all shapes are coloured, a celebration appears!

Categories

  • Scenes – 18 detailed scenes (beach, farm, jungle, space, etc.)
  • Simple – 16 simple shapes for younger children (balloons, car, elephant, etc.)
  • Tessellation – 9 geometric tessellation patterns with musical sounds
  • Wierd – 9 unusual abstract patterns with musical sounds
  • Animals – 9 real animals (ape, giraffe, shark, etc.)
  • Fantasy – 10 magical creatures (dragons, dinosaurs, unicorns, etc.)
  • Young – 14 friendly pictures (boy, dog, lion, castle, etc.)
  • Zap Kids – 5 fun character pictures

The Palette Bar

The palette bar is on the right side of the screen. From top to bottom:

  • Colour buttons (8 colours) – tap to select a colour. The selected colour has a white border.
  • Current colour strip – a thin strip showing which colour is selected.
  • Tool buttons – described below.

Buttons

ButtonLocationAction
HomeTop-leftReturns to the start screen.
SoundTop-rightToggles sound on or off.
UndoPalette barRestores the picture to the state before your last colour fill.
Load PicturePalette barOpens the picture chooser to switch to a different picture.
ClearPalette barResets all shapes back to white (you can undo this).

Sounds

Each colour plays a unique sound when you fill a shape. Different categories have different sound sets:

  • Scenes – various fun sound effects
  • Simple, Animals, Young – playful electronic sounds
  • Tessellation – musical notes
  • Wierd, Fantasy, Zap Kids – musical melodies

Hovering over palette colours also plays their sound as a preview.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Rewards

When you colour every shape in a picture (no white shapes remaining), a celebration animation appears with falling coloured stars and a "Well Done!" message. Tap anywhere or wait a few seconds to dismiss it and continue colouring.

Accessibility

  • Works with mouse, touchscreen, and keyboard.
  • All buttons have descriptive labels for screen readers.
  • Keyboard shortcuts allow full control without a mouse.
  • High contrast palette colours are easy to distinguish.

Tips

  • Use keyboard numbers 0-7 for quick colour switching.
  • If you make a mistake, press Ctrl-Z to undo immediately.
  • Try the Tessellation and Wierd categories to make music while colouring!
  • Your colouring is saved automatically and shown in the picture chooser.
